Josip Stanešić ranked 12th in the world and won the Medal of Excellence.

WorldSkills Competition - Kazan 2019

After a year of preparation for the WorldSkills Kazan 2019 competition, where 1300 participants from around the world competed, the effort truly paid off. Josip, a third-year undergraduate student of Systems Engineering, secured the 12th place in the skill of IT Networks and System Administration and won the Medal of Excellence! Congratulations to Josip and his mentors who prepared him for this competition – Head of Systems Engineering Studies Silvije Papić, Head of the Department for Operating Systems Vedran Dakić, and Assistant Jasmin Redžepagić. Congratulations also to our third-year Software Engineering student Josip Horvat and his mentor Goran Đambić, Head of the Software Engineering Department, for winning 16th place in the skill of IT Software Solutions for Business.
